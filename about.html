Timeline

Late 2020

The idea of DosPro is started by Tim and Ruben. The idea was not acted on, Ruben had prior experience with computer technology and Tim had basic HTML knowledge. Ruben had previously started Knijn Patches, a now subsidiary of DosPro. Tim had previously started Sandman Studios, a text adventure game developer, also a subsidiary.


February 2021

Tim and Ruben decide to actually act on the idea and start DosPro Computers. Justin and Ruben do Head Programming, with Tim doing minor work in coding, Tim also does marketing.

ItemDownloader.exe and AutoClicker.exe are the first products made by DosPro. These were made by Knijn Patches.

The DosPro website is created.
